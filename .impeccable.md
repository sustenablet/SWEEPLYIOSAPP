# SWEEPLY Design Context

## Users
Solo cleaning professionals and small cleaning business owners managing their operations from a mobile device. Users are often on-the-go, may have wet hands, and need quick, efficient workflows. The app replaces spreadsheets, sticky notes, and scattered paperwork.

## Brand Personality
**Clean, Trustworthy, Practical** — A warm, professional aesthetic that reflects the personal nature of cleaning businesses. Not cold or corporate, but approachable and reliable.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Light & Dark mode** — Both supported with adaptive colors
- **Warm stone backgrounds** — Avoids cold corporate blues
- **Card-based layout** — Information organized in clean, tappable cards
- **Generous spacing** — Easy to read and interact with on the go
- **System fonts** with monospace for numbers, currency, and dates

### Color Palette
- **sweeplyAccent** (Blue-Gray): Primary actions, interactive elements
- **sweeplyNavy** (Slate Charcoal): Text, tab bar, dark UI chrome
- **sweeplyBackground** (Warm Stone): Page/scroll backgrounds
- **sweeplySurface** (Bone White): Cards and sheet backgrounds
- **sweeplySuccess** (Teal): Success states, positive affirmations
- **sweeplyWarning** (Warm Amber): Warnings, attention indicators
- **sweeplyDestructive** (Deep Coral): Errors, critical alerts

### Typography
- Display: System default, bold for headings
- Monospace: Currency, invoice numbers, dates
- Size scale: xs, sm, md, base, lg, xl, xxl, xxxl

### Corner Radius
- Small (8pt): Buttons, small elements
- Medium (12pt): Cards, inputs
- Large (16pt): Sheets, modals
- Full (999pt): Pills, badges

## Design Principles

1. **Warm over cold** — Always prefer warm tones over corporate blues
2. **Function first** — Design for efficiency and quick interactions
3. **Clear hierarchy** — Typography should guide the eye naturally
4. **Respect the space** — Generous spacing, no cluttered interfaces
5. **Adaptive excellence** — Dark mode must maintain the warm aesthetic
